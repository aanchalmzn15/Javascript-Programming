Prevention of Pollution

1. Reduce,Reuse,Recycle:-

Minimize waste by reducing consumption,reusing materials,and recycling wherever possible.

2. Use Renewable Energy:-

Switch to solar,wind, or other renewable energy sources to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

3. Promote Public Transport:-

Use public transportation, carpool, of adopt eco-friendly alternatives like bicycles and electric vehicles to cut down vehicular enissions.

4. Plant Trees:-

Increase greenery to absorb carbon dioxide and improve air quality.

5. Proper Waste Management:-

Dispose of waste responsibly, and avoid littering.Encourage composting for organic waste.

6. Avoid Plastic:-

Reduce the use of plastic and opt for biodegradable or reusable alternatives.
