2. Environmental Damage:-


Climate change, global warming, and depletion of the ozone layer are direct consequences of pollution.
Destruction of ecosystems, rivers,forests, and natural habitats.

3. Loss of Biodiversity:-


Pollution causes the extinctionof various species, distuption the ecological balance.
